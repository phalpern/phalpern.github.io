JOB OPPORTUNITY

Sales Position: CLI Development Software

If this is You:

  • A Sales or Business Development professional
  • With experience selling software (especially software tools aimed at engineering developers)
  • Likes to hit the ground running with an existing product
  • Is self-directed
  • Can work part-time or full-time, from home

Then we may have just the position for you.

Halpern-Wight Software, Inc. is a small software development and consulting company based in southern New Hampshire. We have developed a software product called CLI Power Tools that is intended to help companies develop a Cisco-like Command Line Interface (CLI) for their networking hardware. President Pablo Halpern has 20 years of experience in software engineering with a focus on proprietary languages (compilers and interpreters) and network management applications, and is a published author. More information about Pablo Halpern and CLI Power Tools is available on the Web at http://www.halpernwightsoftware.com/CLI.

Customers are start-up companies building medium to large IP routers, switches and similar products, who want to present their customers with a familiar look and feel, but without expending large amounts of development resources on the project. CLI Power Tools can reduce the cost of building a CLI by 50% or more and produces an efficient, robust, and professional CLI. CLI Power Tools has little competition.

This is a commission-based sales position that can be done on a full-time or part-time basis. While training in the use of the product will be provided, you will be mostly self-directed, and have the opportunity to work from home. You will be responsible for closing sales leads generated by prospects who have already expressed some interest in CLI Power Tools, as well as generating new leads. You may be asked to demonstrate the product and answer high-level technical questions.

Requirements:

 The ideal candidate is outgoing and amiable, able to get the product noticed, and able to get customers to commit to purchase. Background in the technical areas of router/switch management and a degree in an engineering discipline is a plus. Send resumes to: phalpern@halpernwightsoftware.com, or phone (603) 801-4558.
